Use of the regional climate risk assessment in risk management and climate adaptation policy
A comprehensive climate risk assessment is necessary to address these evolving hazards and to manage risk in the future! With a clear understanding of the prioritised risks, strategies are developed to manage and mitigate them. This may involve preventive measures such as Nature-based Solutions, contingency plans, or other actions to reduce the impact of the identified risks and adapt to them.
