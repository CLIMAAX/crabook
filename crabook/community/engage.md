# Engage with other regions


## Join the Community of Practice

The [**CLIMAAX Community of Practice**](https://www.climaax.eu/community-of-practice/) (CoP) is a collaborative network designed to foster learning, exchange of knowledge, and collaboration among practitioners, experts, and stakeholders engaged in Climate Risk Assessment at local and regional levels across Europe.

Find information on upcoming and past activities and how to join on the website linked above.

## Reach out via the Handbook forum

The [**GitHub discussion space of the Handbook**](https://github.com/CLIMAAX/crabook/discussions) is set up as a forum open to everyone.
Posts can be read without an account and all registered GitHub users can post.

The forum gives you the opportunity to interact with other Handbook users and the CLIMAAX team:

- **Announcements and news**: updates from the Handbook maintainers
- **Community insights**: ask other practitioners and discuss challenges and good practices
- **Events and opportunities**: share links to CRA-related events and funding opportunities
- **Framework**: all about the CLIMAAX CRA Framework
- **Participation and risk communication**: how to disseminate results and engage with stakeholders, experts and priority groups
- **Regional perspectives**: showcase your own story and share your experience with local CRA
- **Workflows: methods and datasets**: all about quantitative risk analysis workflows and datasets for hazard, exposure and vulnerability
- **Workflows: technical help**: support for running workflows, Python problems

Participation in the forum is subject to our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/CLIMAAX/crabook/blob/main/CONDUCT.md).
Please note that it is not possible to send direct messages to other users on the GitHub platform.
